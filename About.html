About Pixel Photography

Pixel Photography is a creative studio based in Tamil Nadu, dedicated to capturing your most precious moments with perfection. From weddings and portraits to toddler shoots and commercial projects, we bring a blend of artistry, storytelling, and professionalism to every frame.

With thousands of successful shoots, our mission is to turn emotions into everlasting memories. We believe every picture tells a story — and we capture yours with passion, precision, and creativity.

Meet the Founder

Hi, I’m Matt Henry — the lead photographer at Pixel Photography with 10+ years of experience. Whether it's portraits, weddings, or creative shoots, I focus on bringing emotions alive through my lens. My goal is simple — make every client feel comfortable and deliver stunning, high-quality photos they’ll cherish forever.



Our Vision

We aim to capture real moments, real emotions, and natural expressions. Every frame is crafted to tell your story, your personality, and your journey. Photography is not just a profession — it’s our passion.
